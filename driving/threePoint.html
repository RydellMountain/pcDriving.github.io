Three point turn

Number   Steps for Three point turn
1) Check rear-view mirrors and the blind spots.
2) Signal intention (right turn).
3) Select the correct gear.
4) Obtain clutch control (manual).
5) Observe 360 degrees (Rear-view mirrors and blind spot if applicable).
6) Release the handbrake.
7) Move off driving forwards and turn the steering wheel as far right as possible.
8) Counter-steer in the other direction as you stop.
9) Stop.
10) Apply the handbrake.
11) Check the rear-view mirrors and the blind spot to the left.
12) Signal intention.
13) Select reverse gear.
14) Obtain clutch control (manual).
15) Observe 360 degrees (Rear-view mirrors and blind spot if applicable).
16) Release handbrake.
17) Move off in a backward direction and turn the steering wheel to the left.
18) Counter steer to straighten out the wheels.
19) Stop.
20) Apply handbrake.
21) Check the rear-view mirrors and the blind spot to the left.
22) Signal intension.
23) Select gear.
24) Obtain clutch control (manual).
25) Observe 360 degrees (Rear-view mirrors and blind spot if applicable).
26) Release handbrake.
27 Move off driving forwards, and keep left..
28) Cancel signal.
