Parallel Parking

  Steps for Parallel Parking
1) Check the mirrors and the blind spot.
2) Signal intention.
3) Select reverse gear
4) Obtain clutch control (manual).
5) Observe 360 degrees (Rear-view mirrors and blind spot if applicable).
6) Release the handbrake.
7) Move off.
8) Check the blind spot (right blind spot if parking to the left; left blind spot if parking to the right).
9) Reverse steer into the bay without touching any obstacles.
10) Counter-steer if necessary.
11) Stop.
12) Apply the handbrake.
13) Select neutral or park
14) Cancel the signal.
